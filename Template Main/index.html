Give Your Project a
Second Chance.

Relaunch_It provides a second-best chance that empowers developers and communities to revive failed or abandoned crypto projects. Whether it was a rugged pull, mismanaged launch, or bad timing, we provide the platform, exposure, and support to try again. Ours is an organized Community-Take-Over (CTO) scheme with integrity and listing benefit in Relaunch_It Exchange.

How It Works

Project Submission

Submit your failed project for relaunch, and our team will vet it for transparency and potential. Chat With Us.

Relaunch Support & Upgrade

We'll assist with the fair tokenomics redesign, social relaunch campaigns, community feedback integration, and DEX and upcoming in-house exchange listing.

Relaunch Events

Every Friday at 20:00 UTC, we spotlight selected projects with a fresh start and announce their relaunch schedules.

Future Listing

Revived and relaunched projects that maintain at least a $200,000 market cap post-relaunch will be eligible for listing on the Relaunch_It Exchange, our dedicated platform for memecoins and community tokens.

Our Story

We are a team of builders, traders, and community managers who’ve witnessed too many promising projects fade away. We believe in second chances and are committed to creating a safe space for comebacks. Relaunch_It is more than a CEX project – it’s a movement that aims to derisk revival projects through community involvement, developer accountability, and infrastructure that supports success.
Let's rewrite the story of failed tokens together. With Relaunch_It, we can collectively redeem failed project's potential, innovate and improve revived and relaunched projects, and finish what someone elsewhere started with better execution
Join us in building a future where failure isn't the end.
